🎯 Our Mission

Democratizing Student Opportunities Across India

In India, students at tier-1 colleges have 10× more access to opportunities than students at tier-2 and tier-3 colleges — simply because of geography and information asymmetry. A student at IIT Mumbai hears about 20 hackathons a month. A student at a college in Kakinada hears about 1 or 2.

FestNest is built to fix this. By centralizing every campus event into one platform with powerful search and equal access, we ensure a student in Kakinada gets the same information as one in Mumbai.

We believe every talented student deserves a chance to shine, compete, and win — regardless of which college they attend or which city they live in.
